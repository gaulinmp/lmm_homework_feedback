# Excel vs Python

Compare the strengths and weaknesses of Excel and Python for data analytics in accounting.

## Requirements
- Must identify Excel's strength in quick viewing and editing, handling small datasets, or basic modeling capabilities.
- Must identify Python's strength in automation, large datasets, advanced modeling capabilites, or flexibility of capabilities.

## Hints
- Think about which tool handles 10 million rows better.
- Which tool is easier to just open and look at, and edit a 10-row table?
- Which tool can handle both data scraping and report generation?

## Solution Explanation
Excel is great for quick exploration, ad-hoc analysis, and small datasets due to its ubiquitous adoption in Accounting. Python is better at handling larger datasets, enforcing reproducible outputs (being in code), automating repetitive tasks, and building advanced machine learning models, and being the "glue" between disparate systems and parts of a whole solution.
