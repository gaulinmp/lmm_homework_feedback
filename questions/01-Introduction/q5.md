# Analytics in Tax

What are some examples of how data analytics is changing tax accounting?

## Requirements
Must mention one or more of:
    * Optimize tax planning strategies
    * Predict tax impacts of business changes
    * Identify tax credits
    * Automate data extraction, e.g. K-1s, 1099s
    * Estimate tax position risk
    * Better forecasting of tax expenses

## Hints
- How can visualizing data help a tax department?
- Think about tracking where revenue is generated globally.
- 

## Solution Explanation
In tax accounting, data analytics is used to model the tax implications of different business decisions (e.g., supply chain changes), simulate and optimize tax planning strategies, and automate the extraction of tax-relevant data from client documents.
