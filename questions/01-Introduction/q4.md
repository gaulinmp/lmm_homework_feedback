# Analytics in Audit

What are some examples of how data analytics is changing the audit profession?

## Requirements
Must mention one or more of:
    * Moving from sampling to full population testing
    * Better identify anomalies or fraud more effectively
    * Better identifying and quantify risk
    * Automate data acquisition, e.g. OCR, API data access

## Hints
- Think about how many transactions an auditor used to look at versus now.
- What happens when you can test 100% of journal entries?

## Solution Explanation
Data analytics allows auditors to move away from random sampling to testing entire populations of transactions. This comprehensive approach helps in better identifying anomalies, assessing risks, and detecting potential fraud, ultimately increasing audit quality. Advanced analytical capabilities also allow for better anomoly detection to facilitate "audit by exception." Better AI capabilities are also allowing for more automation of tasks like invoice processing.
