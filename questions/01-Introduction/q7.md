# Financial Analytics

What are some examples of data analytics used in financial accounting?

## Requirements

Must mention one or more of:
    * Faster closing proceedures through automation and data processing
    * Enhanced and more comprehensive compliance testing
    * More complex modeling for Financial Statement Analysis (FSA)
    * More comprehensive and complex stakeholder analysis, e.g. investor sentiment

## Hints
- What kind of tasks generally fall under the financial accounting umbrella?
- What kind of things could be adopted to speed up the financial close?

## Solution Explanation
Financial accounting analytics increasingly uses data analytics and automation to improve the speed and quality of financial reporting and analysis, compliance testing, risk estimation and analysis, and conduct market and investor research.
