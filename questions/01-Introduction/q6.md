# Managerial Analytics

What are some examples of data analytics used in management accounting?

## Requirements

Must mention one or more of:
    * More complex models for cost estimation
    * Better forecasting models
    * Analysis of larger datasets
    * Compilation of increasingly complex internal and external datasets
    * Conducting simulations, and "what-if" analyses

## Hints
- What kind of tasks generally fall under the managerial accounting umbrella? Which of those tasks benefit from better data and analytics?
- What kind of data and approaches are used to set budgets?

## Solution Explanation
Managerial accountants use data analytics for internal, often forward-looking, decision-making. Examples include cost estimation, cost optimization, performance measurement, simulations and "what-if" analyses, and budgeting.
